
# 🎓 Research Internship Resources by Kushanavo Rakshit

> A curated collection of resources to help students find and secure
> research internships — professor lists, govt programmes, and more.

---

## 🚀 Why This Repo?

Finding a research internship as an undergrad is overwhelming.
Professors don't reply. Portals are scattered. Deadlines are missed.

So I compiled everything in one place — professor contacts,
government schemes, NPTEL opportunities, and more.

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. **Cold Emailing Professors** — Use the IIT professor xlsx,
   filter by your department/interest area, and send tailored emails
2. **Govt Schemes** — Check the PDF for deadlines and apply early;
   most open between January–March
3. **Formal Programmes** — Use the Research Intern Programmes PDF
   to identify structured programmes with stipends and certificates

---

## ✉️ Cold Email Tips

- Keep it under 150 words
- Mention a specific paper of theirs you read
- Attach your CV (1 page max)
- Follow up once after 2 weeks if no reply
- Subject line: `Research Internship Inquiry — [Your Name] | [College]`
